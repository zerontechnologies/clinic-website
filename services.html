Skin & Hair Services

Comprehensive dermatology and cosmetology services tailored to your skin type and concern.

Acne & Acne Scar Management

Acne is a common condition but, if not treated correctly, can lead to long-lasting scars and pigmentation. Dr. Bist evaluates the grade of acne, triggers and skin type before deciding on a treatment plan.

  • Medical treatment with topical and oral medicines
  • Chemical peels to reduce active acne and marks
  • Guidance on skin care routine and diet
  • Scar treatment options based on depth and type of scars

Fungal Infections

Recurrent fungal infections of the skin, nails and scalp are increasingly common. Correct diagnosis and adequate treatment duration are essential to prevent relapse.

  • Evaluation of extent and severity of infection
  • Appropriate antifungal medicines and creams
  • Counselling on hygiene, clothing and preventive steps

Anti-Aging & Pigmentation

Sun exposure, hormones and lifestyle can cause pigmentation and early signs of aging. The clinic offers medical and minimally invasive options to restore an even skin tone and glow.

  • Treatments for melasma, tanning and uneven skin tone
  • Anti-aging regime tailored to your age and skin type
  • Maintenance plans to protect results

Minor Procedures & Dermato-Surgery

Certain skin growths need safe removal for cosmetic or medical reasons. Procedures are done with proper aseptic precautions and post-care guidance.

  • Removal of moles, warts and skin tags
  • Treatment for ingrown nails and small cysts
  • Cryotherapy and cautery for selected lesions

Hair & Scalp Disorders

Evaluation and management of hair fall, dandruff and other scalp conditions with a focus on finding underlying causes such as nutrition, hormones or lifestyle factors.
