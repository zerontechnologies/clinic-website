Our Medical Services

From everyday illnesses to long‑term conditions, we offer connected care for your whole family.

General Medicine & Family Practice

First point of contact for most health concerns. Our doctors manage fevers, infections, blood pressure, diabetes, headaches, digestive issues and more, with a focus on long‑term control.

  • Consultations for adults and seniors
  • Management of lifestyle diseases
  • Guidance on diet, exercise and sleep
  • Referrals to higher centers when needed

Women & Child Health

Dedicated care for women and children, covering routine check‑ups, vaccinations and common concerns like growth, nutrition and adolescent health.

  • Well‑baby and well‑woman visits
  • Immunization guidance and schedules
  • Management of common pediatric illnesses
  • Counselling on nutrition and development

Skin & Hair Clinic

Evaluation and treatment for common skin, hair and nail conditions such as acne, allergies, infections and hair fall, with an emphasis on safe, long‑term solutions.

  • Treatment for acne, rashes and allergies
  • Care for hair fall and scalp issues
  • Advice on daily skin and hair routines

Diagnostics & Day Care

On‑site basic investigations help your doctor diagnose and treat faster, often in a single visit.

  • Routine blood and urine tests (via partner labs)
  • ECG and basic vital monitoring
  • Dressings, injections and IV fluids (as appropriate)

Preventive Health Check‑Ups

Periodic health assessments that track key health markers, identify risks early and help you stay on top of your overall wellbeing.

Need to consult a doctor?

Call the clinic or request an appointment online. Our team will help you choose a suitable time.
