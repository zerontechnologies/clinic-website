About Dr. J.S. Bist

Qualified and renowned Skin Doctor in Dehradun with over two decades of experience.

Dr J.S. Bist

Dr Jitendra Singh Bisht is a qualified and renowned Skin Doctor in Dehradun. He holds an MD degree in Skin & VD with over two decades of rich clinical and teaching experience.

Dr Bist has previously served as Professor & Head at Shri Mahant Indresh Hospital, Dehradun. During his tenure, he has trained numerous postgraduate students and handled a wide spectrum of dermatological conditions.

He is currently practicing independently at his own skin care clinic located opposite Doon Hospital, Dehradun, providing ethical, evidence-based treatments for skin, hair and nail disorders.

Qualification

Post Graduate in Dermatology, Venereology and Leprology (DVL) from Baba Raghavdas Medical College, Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh (2003).

Recent Position Held

Professor and Head, Department of Dermatology, Shri Mahant Indresh Hospital, Dehradun.

Current Practice

Dr. Bist is presently consulting at his Skin Care Clinic located opposite Doon Hospital, Dehradun.

Clinical Interests

  • Pigmentation and melasma
  • Psoriasis and eczema
  • Hair fall and scalp disorders
